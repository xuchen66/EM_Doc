18. Finishing Up

When you are done with your session, perform finishing up procedure.

  • Specimen rod out.

  • Reset Stage Position, X, Y, Z and A.

  • Filament 0.

  • H.T. OFF.

  • Cryo-cycle, normally for 2-3 hours.

  • Data display OFF.

  • Display OFF.

  • Log your session on logbook.
